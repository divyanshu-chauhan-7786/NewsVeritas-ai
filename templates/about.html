About TruthLens

TruthLens is an advanced AI-based web application designed to detect whether a given news article is real or fake. It uses state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning techniques to analyze the credibility of online content.

By leveraging transformer-based deep learning models like RoBERTa, our system identifies patterns, linguistic cues, and semantic meaning in news text to make intelligent predictions with high accuracy.

Our Mission

To combat the growing problem of fake news and misinformation across digital media platforms by providing reliable, AI-powered verification tools.

Objective

Help users verify the authenticity of news articles before believing or sharing them, promoting a more informed and responsible online community.

How It Works

1

Input News Text

User enters or pastes a news article or headline into the application

2

AI Processing

RoBERTa model analyzes the text using advanced NLP techniques

3

Pattern Recognition

Model identifies linguistic patterns and contextual features

4

Result Delivery

System provides prediction (Real/Fake) with confidence score

Key Features

Real-time Analysis

Instant detection of fake news with live processing

Confidence Scoring

AI-Powered Accuracy
